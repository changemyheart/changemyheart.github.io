Session 8 Vowel Overview, I and EE

Posted on 2017-09-16 | Post modified: 2017-09-19

The best way to learn American vowels is by Ear Training. Listen carefully and repeat.

Front Vowels:(From high to low)

EE  - as in  Heat
I    - as in  Hit
AE  - as in  Hate
EH  - as in  Het (nonsense word)
A   - as in   Hat

Back Vowels: (From high to low)

OO  - as in  Boot
U   - as in  Book
OW  - as in  Boat
AW  - as in  Bought
AH  - as in  Bot (nonsense word)

Comparing Heat (EE) and Hit (I)

Remember,…
    Heat is high
    Hit is lower

Heat – Hit
Keen – Kin
Deal – Dill
Seek – Sick
Seen – Sin
Reap – Rip
Teal - Till
Bean –Been

Bean – I ate a bean (noun). – ‘high’
Been – I have been here (verb). – ‘low’
